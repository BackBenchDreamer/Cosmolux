What is an Orrery?

+

An orrery is a mechanical model of the solar system, illustrating the relative positions and motions of the planets and moons. Cosmolux brings this concept to life, focusing on the dynamic movement of Near-Earth Objects.

The Threat of NEOs

+

Near-Earth Objects pose potential threats to our planet, with over 34,000 known asteroids crossing Earth's orbital path. Understanding their trajectories is crucial for planetary defense strategies.
